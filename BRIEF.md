# P303 — Mobile Experience Case Study

## Project Overview

CareCheck is a mobile-first symptom check-in experience designed to help patients quickly understand what to do next when they are not feeling well.

The experience is built specifically for mobile context: quick interactions, minimal typing, and clear next steps, supporting users who may be distracted, anxious, or multitasking.

---

## User

**Primary User:**  
A patient or caregiver managing health concerns on their phone

**Scenario:**  
The user is experiencing symptoms (e.g., fever, fatigue, cough) and wants to quickly determine whether to rest, message a provider, schedule an appointment, or seek urgent care.

**Context of Use:**
- On a mobile device
